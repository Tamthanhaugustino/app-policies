Privacy Policy — Trivia Quiz: General Knowledge

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This app is designed to respect your privacy. In short: we do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not ask for your name, email, location, or contacts, and it contains no advertising and no third-party analytics.

Data stored on your device

Your quiz scores and progress are saved locally on your device only, using the device's standard app storage. This information never leaves your tablet, is never uploaded to any server, and is removed if you uninstall the app.

Internet access

The app works fully offline and does not require an internet connection to play. It does not transmit any data over the network.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Because it collects no personal information, it does not knowingly gather data from children or anyone else.

Changes to this policy
